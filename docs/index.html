Welcome to Yahtzee for the Atari 2600

Controls:

  • F12 - Reset/Restart the game.
  • Arrow keys - like the joystick.
  • Space bar - the firebutton. Perform the action.

The top zone is your scoreble area. Any line totally blank may be scored.

The middle zone are the dice. Use left and right to select with dice to re-roll.

The bottom zone enables the re-roll of the selected dice. You get three rolls total.
